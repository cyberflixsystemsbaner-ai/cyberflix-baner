Frequently Asked Questions

How do I identify my laptop model number?

The model number is usually printed on the bottom label of the laptop or shown in the system information section.

Are all RAM modules compatible with every laptop?

No. RAM compatibility depends on generation, memory type, supported capacity, speed and available slots.

What information is needed for a laptop battery?

Share the laptop model and, where possible, the battery part number printed on the existing battery.

Do display panels differ between laptop models?

Yes. Display size, resolution, connector position, connector type, mounting points and refresh rate can differ.
